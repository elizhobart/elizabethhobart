Welcome!

I am a rare materials cataloger with 15 years' worth of experience. I have cataloged books from incunables through the present, as well as cuneiform tablets, medieval and modern manuscripts, scores, sound recordings in every format, DVDs, and maps. Since 2022, I have also served as the Interim Head of Cataloging and Metadata Services at Penn State, directing the work of 25 catalogers serving 22 campuses.
